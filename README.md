# AquaCrop-model-simulation
This repository contains AquaCrop model simulations and analysis scripts used to estimate  irrigation water demand under different climate change scenarios. The work supports efficient water resource management and irrigation planning in agricultural systems.
The goal of this project is to quantify crop water requirements and irrigation water demand using the FAO AquaCrop model and interpret the results for different scenarios.
# Objectives
to evaluate the historical and projected changes in irrigation water demand for rice production across Taiwan 
To assess future changes in irrigation water demand with designed adaptive strategies (single and double cropping season management) under future climate scenarios 
To identify optimal planting dates that synchronize irrigation water demand with favorable climate conditions for the future
